Bio

I am a second year Ph.D. student at the Max Planck ETH Center for Learning Systems (CLS), advised by Florian Dörfler at ETH Zürich and Michael Muehlebach at the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems, Tübingen.

I received my master’s degree in Information Science and Technology from the University of Tokyo, Japan, in 2024. During my master's program, I was also an exchange student at ETH Zürich from 2022 until 2023. I received my bachelor's degree in Mathematical Engineering and Information Physics from the University of Tokyo in 2022.

My research interests include the intersection and the interplay between system theory, optimization and machine learning.
